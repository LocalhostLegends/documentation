HR Platform

A modern web portal for automating HR management processes.

About the platform​

The platform ensures effective interaction between the HR department and employees, simplifies recruiting, onboarding, personnel management, and analytics.

Key Features​

  • 🚀 Employee Management - the full cycle from hiring to firing
  • 📝 Recruiting - job and candidate management
  • 📊 Analytics - HR dashboards and reports
  • 🔐 Security - role-based access model

Technologies​

  • Backend: NestJS, PostgreSQL, TypeORM
  • Auth: JWT, Argon2
  • Docs: Docusaurus
